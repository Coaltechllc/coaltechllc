Commercial Subcontracting Support

Coal Tech LLC partners with licensed commercial and industrial contractors to provide specialized welding and workforce training support. Subcontracted services are coordinated in compliance with jurisdictional licensing requirements.

Subcontracted Welding Capabilities

  • On-site commercial and industrial welding
  • Emergency welding response
  • MIG (GMAW), TIG (GTAW), SMAW (Stick), FCAW (Flux-Core)
  • OFC cutting and structural repairs
  • Code-aware, safety-focused execution

Workforce Training & Upskilling

Hands-on and customized welding training programs for corporate and commercial clients. We provide task-specific training and refresher sessions to help companies maintain, improve, or standardize OFC cutting, GMAW (MIG), GTAW (TIG), SMAW (stick), and FCAW (flux-core) techniques for their workforce. Prioritizing hands-on job site safety, code awareness, proper setup, and production-quality technique.

Out-of-State Project Coordination

For projects located outside Washington State, services may be provided only when subcontracted by a properly licensed contractor operating in that jurisdiction. Availability is evaluated on a project-by-project basis.
