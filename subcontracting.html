Frequently Asked Questions

Can Coal Tech LLC work on secure or restricted facilities?

Yes. Coal Tech LLC has experience working in secure and restricted facilities and can meet site access requirements, safety protocols, and background screening procedures as required by the client or governing authority.

Is Coal Tech LLC clearance-ready for government or military base projects?

Coal Tech LLC has previously been assigned to projects on U.S. Army, Navy, and Air Force installations. Our team is able to comply with access control, credentialing, and clearance processes required for government and military sites.

Do you perform welding on fire-rated doors and frames?

Yes. We perform welding repairs and modifications on fire-rated doors, frames, and hollow metal openings. All work is completed with consideration for applicable fire and life safety requirements and project specifications.

Do you provide hollow metal door and frame fabrication and repair?

Yes. We specialize in fabrication, reinforcement, and repair services for hollow metal doors and frames used in commercial, industrial, and secure facilities.

What types of clients do you typically work with?

Coal Tech LLC works with general contractors, subcontractors, facility managers, property managers, industrial operators, and government entities on commercial and industrial welding and metal fabrication projects.
